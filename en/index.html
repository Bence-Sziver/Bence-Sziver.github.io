Welcome to the website of the MTA LENDÜLET Plant NaNObiology Research Group!

The Plant NaNObiology Research Group was established in 2023 with the support of the MTA Lendület program. Our goal is to study the possibilities of enhancing drought tolerance in plants using nanomaterials within the framework of our project from 2023 to 2028.
